# 🗺️ Integrating Your Custom Map Image

## Quick Setup

Your invitation builder already supports custom map images! Here's how to use your Khmer location map:

### **Step 1: Prepare Your Map Image**

- Your map image should be a clean, clear image (PNG or JPG works best)
- Recommended dimensions: 600px × 400px (or similar aspect ratio)
- File size: Keep it under 500KB for optimal performance

### **Step 2: Upload to the Map Section**

1. Open the **Invitation Builder** → scroll to the **🗺️ Location** section
2. You'll see two fields:
   - **Google Maps Link**: Paste your Google Maps URL (optional)
   - **រូបភាពផែនទី (Map Image)**: Click to upload your custom map

3. Click the file input and select your map image
4. The preview will appear below showing your map

### **Step 3: In the Invitation Preview**

The map will automatically display in the live preview (right panel) with:

- "Location" or "ទីតាំង" heading in your chosen heading color
- Your custom map image with rounded corners and custom border color
- Centered and responsive layout

## 📝 Map Image Recommendations

### Good Map Formats:

- ✅ **Hand-drawn line maps** (like yours) - Clean, elegant look
- ✅ **Street maps** - From Google Maps screenshots
- ✅ **Custom illustrated maps** - Branded or stylized
- ✅ **Venue layouts** - Floor plans or building diagrams

### Pro Tips:

1. **Contrast**: Make sure text is readable against the background
2. **Khmer Labels**: Include Khmer text for better localization
3. **Color Matching**: Choose colors that complement your invitation theme
4. **Resolution**: Use high-resolution images for print-quality invitations

## 🎨 Customization

- The map border color automatically matches your **Heading Color** (from the Design section)
- Adjust heading color in the **🎨 Design** section to match your map aesthetic
- The map displays with a max-width for mobile optimization

## 📱 Responsive Design

- Maps automatically scale for mobile and desktop views
- The image maintains aspect ratio on all screen sizes
- Maximum width constraint ensures readability

## 🔗 Linking to Location

1. In the **Google Maps Link** field, paste your Google Maps URL
2. When guests click the map in the digital invitation, it opens the directions
3. Combine with your custom map image for context

**Your map is ready!** Simply upload it through the editor and watch it appear in real-time in the preview. 🎉
